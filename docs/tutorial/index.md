# Tutorial

!!! info "Coming Soon"
    This tutorial is under active development for the GTC 2026 launch.

Welcome to the Strands Robots tutorial — a 9-part progressive guide that teaches everything from scratch.

## Prerequisites

- Python 3.10+
- `pip install strands-robots`

## Chapters

| # | Chapter | What You'll Learn | Time |
|---|---------|-------------------|------|
| 1 | [Your First Robot](01-your-first-robot.md) | Create a robot, run basic actions | 5 min |
| 2 | [Simulation](02-simulation.md) | MuJoCo basics, scene building | 10 min |
| 3 | [Policies](03-policies.md) | VLA models, inference | 10 min |
| 4 | [AI Agents](04-agents.md) | Natural language control | 10 min |
| 5 | [Multi-Robot](05-multi-robot.md) | Coordinate multiple robots | 10 min |
| 6 | [Recording Data](06-recording.md) | Teleoperation, datasets | 10 min |
| 7 | [Training](07-training.md) | Fine-tune policies | 15 min |
| 8 | [Real Hardware](08-real-hardware.md) | Deploy to physical robots | 15 min |
| 9 | [Advanced](09-advanced.md) | Mesh, custom embodiments | 15 min |

**Total time:** ~2 hours from zero to deploying on real hardware.
